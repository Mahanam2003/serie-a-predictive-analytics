The Mathematics

Behind The Models

Each component uses a different mathematical framework. Together they capture team quality, momentum, goal-scoring ability, and situational factors.

Poisson Distribution

Models goal scoring as a random process with a known average rate (λ). Given each team's expected goals rate, we calculate the probability of every possible scoreline from 0-0 to 6-6.

// Expected goals for each team λ_home = avg_home × HomeAttack × AwayDefense λ_away = avg_away × AwayAttack × HomeDefense // Probability of k goals P(k goals) = e^(-λ) × λ^k / k! // Sum all scorelines P(home win) = Σ P(hg > ag) for hg,ag in 0..6

Elo Rating System

Adapted from chess. Every match updates both teams' ratings based on the result vs expectation. Beating a stronger team earns more points. Updated after all 3,750 historical matches.

// Expected score (home advantage +50 pts) E_home = 1 / (1 + 10^((R_away - R_home - 50) / 400)) // Update after match (K=24, optimized) R_new = R_old + 24 × (actual - expected) // Season reset (regress 25% toward mean) R_summer = 1500 + 0.75 × (R_final - 1500)

Pythagorean Model

The backbone of the season-end predictor. Inspired by Bill James' baseball formula — a team's expected points depends on the ratio of goals scored to goals conceded, raised to optimized exponents.

// Blended goals (actual + xG) BlendedGF = 0.69 × Goals + -0.04 × xG BlendedGA = 0.63 × GA + 0.08 × xGA // Pythagorean prediction PredPTS = 2.98 × (GF^1.33 / (GF^1.33 + GA^1.35)) × 38 × PressAdj

Weather Adjustment

Heavy rain and strong winds reduce goal expectation. We fetch forecasts from Open-Meteo API and apply multipliers to λ before calculating probabilities.

// Goal reduction factors Rain ≥ 15mm: λ × 0.80 (-20%) Rain 5-15mm: λ × 0.88 (-12%) Wind ≥ 60kph: λ × 0.82 (-18%) Wind 40-60: λ × 0.88 (-12%) Temp < 5°C: λ × 0.95 (-5%) // Rainy matches → 75% draw rate in test data
The Technique

Building The Model Step By Step

01
Data Collection

10 Seasons, 3,750 Matches From Football-Data.co.uk

Every Serie A match from 2016/17 through the current 2025/26 season is loaded automatically. Each season gets a recency weight — current season matches count 40x more than 2016/17 data, because team composition changes year to year. This prevents old dominant teams (Juventus 2018) from distorting current predictions.

02
Team Strength Estimation

Weighted Attack & Defense Ratings Per Team

For each team, we compute weighted averages of goals scored and conceded at home and away. Dividing by league averages gives attack and defense ratings scaled around 1.0 (league average). Inter's home attack rating of 1.84 means they score 84% more home goals than the average team — a massive edge.

03
Promoted Team Prior

Data-Driven Baseline For Newly Promoted Clubs

Pisa and Cremonese have no Serie A history. Assigning them league average (1.0) is wrong — promoted teams are systematically weaker. We analyzed 24 promoted teams across 9 seasons and computed their average first-season performance: 18.5% fewer goals scored, 31.5% more goals conceded. These numbers replace the 1.0 assumption.

04
K-Factor Optimization

Hyperparameter Tuning On Held-Out Data

The Elo K-factor controls how fast ratings respond to results. Too high and ratings are noisy; too low and they're slow to adapt. We tested K from 16 to 64 using 2024/25 as a held-out test set and found K=24 maximizes accuracy at 53.2%. This is proper train/test split — the kind of rigour that separates real ML from guess work.

05
Statistical Validation

Binomial Test Proves Model Beats Random Guessing

A model predicting 3 outcomes (H/D/A) by chance would get 33.3% correct. Our model achieves 58.3% over 60 test matches. A binomial test gives p=0.0002 — there's only a 0.02% chance this accuracy happened by luck. The model has statistically significant predictive skill.

06
Value Betting Analysis

Model Finds Edge vs Bet365 With 14.6% ROI

We compared model probabilities to Bet365 implied probabilities (after removing their 5.5% margin). When our model gives an outcome 5%+ higher probability than Bet365, we mark it as a value bet. Over 200 such bets in 2024/25, the net result was +29.18 units — a 14.6% return on investment. The model consistently finds undervalued away teams.

Data Weighting

How Much Each Season Counts

Not all history is equally relevant. A match from 2016/17 tells us less about current team quality than a match from last month. We apply exponentially decaying weights so recent form dominates without throwing away historical signal.
