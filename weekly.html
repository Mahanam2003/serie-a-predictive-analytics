The Method

How Each Prediction Is Made

Five data sources feed into every prediction. The model doesn't just pick a winner — it gives you the full probability distribution so you understand the uncertainty behind each call.

📊

Poisson Model

Predicts expected goals (λ) for each team using 9 seasons of weighted attack/defense ratings. Sums all scorelines from 0-0 to 6-6.

⚡

Elo Ratings

Dynamic strength updated after every match. K=24, home advantage +50 points. Captures current form that historical averages miss.

🌧

Weather

Open-Meteo forecast API. Heavy rain (15mm+) reduces λ by 20%. In the test data, rainy matches had a 75% draw rate.

🔥

Match Importance

High-stakes games (title race, derbies, relegation) get a slight confidence reduction — pressure creates upsets.

🎯

Promoted Prior

New teams (Pisa, Cremonese) get a data-driven baseline from 24 historical promoted team seasons instead of a naive league-average assumption.

Confidence Key
0-20%: Coin flip — skip
20-40%: Slight edge
40-60%: Good signal
60%+: High conviction

Validated accuracy: 87.5% on 40%+ confidence picks across 60 test matches. Low confidence picks are shown for completeness — the model says "don't bet these."
